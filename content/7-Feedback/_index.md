### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at FCAJ is highly dynamic and innovation-driven. Everyone is passionate about cloud computing and AI. The team encourages open discussion and experimentation, which allowed me to freely explore AWS services without the fear of making mistakes. Although the remote collaboration was mostly smooth, having more offline tech-meetups would make the experience even better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentors were incredibly knowledgeable and patient. Instead of giving me the direct answers for my AWS deployment issues or frontend bugs, they guided me to read the right documentation and understand the root causes. The admin team was also very responsive in providing access credentials and cloud resources promptly, ensuring no delays in my learning process.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
As someone pursuing a degree in Software Engineering, this program bridged the gap between academic theories and industry practices perfectly. I got to apply my programming skills to build a real-world Smart Media Analytics application, heavily utilizing AWS serverless architectures which is rarely taught in depth at the university.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This journey was a massive boost to my technical arsenal. I went from having basic knowledge of cloud concepts to confidently deploying scalable frontend applications and integrating them with AI services. I also improved my agile project management skills, code review practices, and technical presentation skills through our weekly syncs.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The culture is built on continuous learning and mutual support. Whether it was a senior cloud architect or a fellow intern, everyone was willing to share their insights. The hackathons and collaborative events really fostered a strong sense of belonging and team spirit.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provided generous AWS credits for experimentation and access to premium learning materials (like AWS Skill Builder). The flexible working hours were also a huge plus, allowing me to balance my university studies while fully engaging with the project.

### Additional Questions
- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
Successfully deploying the Smart Media Analytics project to production and seeing the AI services process our media files in real-time.
- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
Providing a more structured onboarding roadmap for beginners who have absolutely zero prior experience with AWS.
- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
Absolutely! It's one of the best places to get hands-on experience with cutting-edge Cloud and AI technologies while being supported by top-tier professionals.
