# Summary Report: “Cloud Architect”

### Event Objectives

- A competitive learning event where teams compete by answering cloud computing and architecture design questions.
- Questions are arranged from basic to advanced levels, allowing participants to test and strengthen their cloud knowledge in a fun and engaging environment.

### Key Highlights
Team-based competition focused on cloud computing and architecture design.
Questions covering both fundamental and advanced cloud concepts.
Progressive difficulty levels that challenged participants' technical knowledge and problem-solving skills.
Fast-paced tie-breaker rounds that encouraged quick thinking and decision-making.
Collaborative learning through teamwork, discussion, and knowledge sharing.
### Key Takeaways
Strengthened understanding of cloud computing concepts and architecture design principles.
Improved ability to analyze and solve cloud-related scenarios under time constraints.
Enhanced teamwork and communication skills through collaborative problem-solving.
Gained exposure to a wide range of cloud topics in an engaging and competitive environment.
### Event Experience

Participating in the Cloud Architect event was both enjoyable and educational. The competition format created an exciting atmosphere while encouraging participants to apply their cloud knowledge in practice. Through discussions with teammates and exposure to diverse questions, participants were able to reinforce existing knowledge, discover new concepts, and gain a deeper appreciation for cloud architecture and solution design.
